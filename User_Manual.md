# 📖 Auto-Attendance User Quick-Start Guide

Welcome to your Church Attendance Management System. This guide covers the essential workflows to get you started quickly.

## 1. Initial Setup (Settings)
Before you start registering members, you must configure your church identity.

1.  **Navigate to Settings**: Click the ⚙️ icon in the sidebar.
2.  **Member ID Prefix**: This is CRITICAL. Enter a 2-3 letter code for your church (e.g., `SK` for Skudai, `KL` for Kuala Lumpur).
3.  **Branding**: Upload your **Church Logo** and enter your **Church Name**.
4.  **Save**: Click "Save App Settings" to apply.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> The Prefix prevents ID "crashes" when you merge data from different churches later. Always set it first!

---

## 2. Managing Members
### Registering a New Member
1.  Go to the **Members** page.
2.  Click the teal **+ Add Member** button.
3.  Input the name and details.
4.  **Face Capture**: Click the capture button to take a photo. This is what the AI uses to "recognize" the person at the door.
5.  **Save**: Their ID will be generated automatically (e.g., `SK-0001`).

### Synchronization (Sync In/Out)
To combine databases from two different laptops:
- **On PC 1**: Go to Members, select the records you want, and click **Sync Out**. Save the `.zip` file to a thumb drive.
- **On PC 2**: Go to Members, click **Sync In**, and select that `.zip` file. All photos and data will merge automatically.

---

## 3. Running Attendance
### Starting a Session
1.  Click **Dashboard** in the sidebar.
2.  Select a **Session Type** (e.g., Sunday Service).
3.  Click **Start Attendance**.
4.  The system will begin recognizing faces. If someone is recognized, their name and photo will appear on the "Checked In" list.

### Resuming or Ending
- If you need to pause, you can stop the camera and later click **Resume Session**.
- Click **End Session** when the service is over to close the records.

---

## 4. Reports & Exports
- **Attendance Reports**: Go to the **Reports** page to see who attended specific dates.
- **Bulk Export**: Use the top buttons in Members or Reports to download entire lists as **Excel** or **PDF**.
- **Individual Export**: Hover over the icons in the Member table to see the **📕 PDF Download** icon for a specific person's profile.

---

## 💡 Pro Tips
- **Multi-Search**: In the Members list, you can search by Name, ID, or Area simultaneously.
- **Tooltips**: If you're unsure what an icon does, just point your mouse at it! A description will float over the icon.
- **Backup**: Regularly use the "Create New Backup" button in Settings to keep your data safe.
